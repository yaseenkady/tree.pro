# Epidemiology

Estrogen use
(Increases thyroxine-binding globulin, which take up available thyroxine.)

# Symptom

Feeling cold

Fatigue

Weight gain

Constipation

# Sign

Delayed puberty

Short stature

Dry skin

Bradycardia

Mental status change

# Diagnostic

Blood Free T4
(Low.)

Blood thyroid stimulating hormone
(High.)

Brain function
(In children.)

# Treatment and Management

Thyroxine
