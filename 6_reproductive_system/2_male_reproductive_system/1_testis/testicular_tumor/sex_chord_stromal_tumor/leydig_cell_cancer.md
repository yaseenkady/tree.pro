# Comment

4-5% of germ cell tumor.

Spreads lymphatically to:

- Para-aortic nodes
- Retroperitoneal nodes
- Mediastinal nodes
- Supraclavicular nodes

Spreads hematogeneously to:

- Lungs
- Brain
- Liver
- Bones
