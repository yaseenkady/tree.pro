# Comment

Germ cell cancers are classified into 2:

- Germ cell neoplasia in situ
- Not germ cell neoplasia in situ

Spreads lymphatically:

- Para-aortic nodes
- Retroperitoneal nodes
- Mediastinal nodes
- Supraclavicular nodes

Spreads hematogeneously:

- Lungs
- Brain
- Liver
- Bones
