# Comment

Neuroendocrine tumor originating in:

- Stomach
- Small intestine
- Appendix
- Colon
- Rectum
- Lung

Slow growing and usually benign.

Commonly makes multiple hormones:

- Insulin
- Gastrin
- Glucagon
- VIP
- Adrenocorticotropic hormone
- Somatostatin
